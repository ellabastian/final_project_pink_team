Information about Food Banks

What is a food bank?

A food bank is usually a charity that distributes free food to people experiencing hardship. They can also provide other items such as toiletries, debt advice, and help accessing support.

Why are they needed?

Food Banks stop people going hungry. In 2020/21 approximately 2.5 million people used a foodbank in the United Kingdom, over 600 thousand more than the previous year.
